About us

Romasanta Films is an independent production company based in Helsinki. We make films, photographs and visual work that prioritise experimentation over convention — projects that are willing to be strange, slow, or formally unusual.

We work across a wide range of formats depending on what each project calls for: digital cinema, 35mm, 16mm, Super 8, infrared, VHS, and medium format photography. The format is never incidental — it shapes the image at a fundamental level.

We also run our own darkroom, handling film developing for both motion picture and still photography across virtually every format.

"Film crafts outside the ordinary for ideas outside the ordinary."
— Romasanta Films
